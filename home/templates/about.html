About FanFirst

A better way to connect true fans with the artists they love

Concert tickets for top artists often sell out in minutes—leaving real fans behind. Our platform introduces a smarter, fairer way to distribute tickets by prioritizing fans based on their actual engagement with the artist.

Why We Built This

Traditional ticketing systems favor speed and bots. This results in scalping, inflated prices, and an unfair experience for true fans. We believe dedicated listeners—the people who support artists the most—deserve better access to live shows.

How It Works

Sign In as a Fan

Link your streaming profiles to let us understand your music preferences.

Engage with Events

Discover upcoming concerts and register your interest.

Get Ranked Fairly

We use your listening history to calculate a fan engagement score and rank you fairly among others.

Priority Tickets

A portion of tickets is reserved for high-ranking fans, so you get a better shot at attending.

For Event Organizers

Host Smarter Events

Create events, set ticket allocation rules, and reward your most loyal fans.

Insights that Matter

Monitor fan engagement, ticket sales, and get real-time insights—all from one dashboard.

Built for True Fans

This platform is for the people who play the same song on repeat, who stream every album drop, and who actually care about the music—not the resale value of a ticket.
